### Purpose of Establishment
Joseph Clinic is a medical institution and non-profit organization providing charitable medical care to the poor and sick. It was founded in 2013 by the late Fr. Choi Young-Sik, Mathias.

The clinic follows the mission of caring for the poor with the love of Christ and providing free medical care to those in need.

### Mission Statement
Helping poor and helpless neighbors is a concrete practice of Christ’s teachings and the mission of the Church.
