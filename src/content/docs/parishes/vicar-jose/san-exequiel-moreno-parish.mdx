Honoring the Spanish Augustinian Recollect missionary Saint Ezekiel Moreno. The parish is well-known for its passionate devotion to its patron saint and lively community events. For the faithful, it offers regular masses, confessions, and religious instruction. The church incorporates aspects of the Catholic tradition of the Philippines. The parish actively provides social services to the community’s most impoverished members. To deepen parishioners’ faith, it plans spiritual retreats, Bible studies, and youth initiatives. San Exequiel’s feast day and local celebrations are enthusiastically and fervently observed. The parish aims to cultivate in its members both social responsibility and spiritual growth.
